About d-span

d-span is an easy-to-use DNA sequence analyzer designed for students and researchers to explore and analyze DNA sequences efficiently. It offers important tools to help understand key biological concepts through interactive DNA analysis.

With d-span, users can search for specific DNA patterns, calculate the GC content of sequences, and generate complementary DNA strands with simple and accurate results.

Key features include:

  • Pattern searching for common codons and custom sequences.
  • GC content calculation to assess DNA stability.
  • Generation of complementary strands based on base pairing rules.

d-span aims to simplify DNA analysis and enhance learning by providing a clear, reliable, and interactive interface.
